Introduction to Data Mining - Simplilearn (Skillup)

This certificate verifies the successful completion of the "Introduction to Data Mining Course" offered by Simplilearn (Online Mode). The course covered essential data mining concepts, data preprocessing techniques, classification and clustering methods, association rule mining, and practical applications in real-world business scenarios. It also introduced industry-relevant tools and techniques to analyze, interpret, and extract meaningful insights from large datasets.

Issued in May 2025, this certification demonstrates a solid understanding of data mining principles and practical applications. Awarded by Simplilearn via its SkillUp platform, the course covered core concepts including data preprocessing, classification, clustering, and association rule mining. Learners gained hands-on knowledge in extracting patterns from data and applying analytical techniques using real-world case studies and tools. The training highlighted problem-solving approaches in business intelligence and data analysis.
